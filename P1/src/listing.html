Massive Lodge With Tall Trees

$712/night - Hosted By: Mark Zuckerberg - - 4.05/5

8 guests - 4 bedrooms, 3 bathrooms

A modern, private lodge secluded within a private 19 acres, just 90 miles away from Southern Ontario as shown in the Toronto Star! This 4 bedroom 3 bathroom lodge was developed in response to the beautiful landscape it is placed within and is surrounded by forest and wildlife, overlooking a beautiful lake.

The lodge comes with High-Speed Fiber Optic Internet along with Ducted A/C & Central Heating. The bedroom arrangements are as follows:

  • Master Bedroom: Queen Bed
  • Bedroom 2: Full Bed
  • Bedroom 3: Full Bed
  • Bedroom 4: Twin Bunk Bed

Please note that this is lodge is appropriate for a maximum of 12 guests, but 8 guests are recommended for optimal comfort.
